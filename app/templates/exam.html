Happy Tails Pet Shop Services

Description of Services Offered

  • Grooming

    Happy Tails Pet Shop's grooming services include bathing, haircuts, nail trimming, and ear cleaning to keep your pets looking and feeling their best.

  • Training

    We offer obedience training classes for dogs of all ages, focusing on basic commands, socialization, and behavioral issues.

  • Veterinary Services

    Our in-house veterinary services include wellness check-ups, vaccinations, and preventative care to keep your pets healthy.
